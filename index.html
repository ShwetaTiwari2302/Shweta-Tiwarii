Beagle: The Perfect Family Dog

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. Beagles are scent hounds, developed primarily for hunting hare. Learn more about the Beagle breed on Wikipedia.

Trivia about Beagles

  1. Beagles are known for their excellent sense of smell.
  2. They are one of the most popular breeds in the United States.
  3. Beagles were bred as hunting dogs, specifically to track rabbits and hare.
  4. They are friendly and curious dogs, making them great family pets.
  5. Famous beagles include Snoopy from the comic strip *Peanuts*.
